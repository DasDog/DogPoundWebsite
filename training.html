Training


As many of you already know, training your pet is very important. Unfortunately it can be difficult at times. Don't fret! We offer classes for both you and your best pal taught by trained professionals. Mr. Killoran (our head trainer) has been in charge of our training program for over a decade and even has experience with top show dogs. In 2006 him and his Saint Bernard won the Westminister Dog Show. He is also a member of the prestigious Saint Bernard Club. Take a look at some of our reviews!
